About AierLab


AierLab is a forward-thinking research group dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence in a way that aligns with human values, privacy, and societal needs. Originating from the transformation of the PAR Group, AierLab has grown into a vibrant community of researchers, students, and industry professionals who collaborate to explore new frontiers in AI.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical applications, ensuring that AI technologies are safe, reliable, and beneficial for all.

Through a combination of foundational and applied research across various AI domains, including machine learning, natural language processing, multi-modal fusion, and AI safety, AierLab seeks to innovate and influence both academia and industry.

We are committed to fostering collaboration and ensuring responsible AI development. Learn more about our projects and how you can get involved on our official website.
